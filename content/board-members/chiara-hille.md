Chiara Hille is an HR Business Partner, internal Coach, and facilitator at EY. With a background in business and economics, she brings strong project management and people-development expertise. As an ICF-certified Coach, she creates safe, supportive spaces for personal and professional growth. Chiara has been on the ICF Denmark board since January 2026, serving as Director of Finance.

**Hvorfor bruger du din tid på ICF Danmark?**

I am motivated to contribute to the community that shaped my own professional and personal growth. Coaching helped me find clarity, direction, and authenticity, and I want to give back by supporting ICF Denmark's mission. For me, serving on the board is a meaningful way to strengthen the Coaching profession and support its impact in Denmark.

**Din rolle i ICF Danmark**

I am the Director of Finance, in my role I ensure transparency, structure, and responsible financial oversight. My focus is to support the board's initiatives and help create a solid foundation for ICF Denmark's continued growth and sustainable financial foundation.

**Hvordan kan medlemmerne hjælpe?**

In my role as Board Member, I am always looking for ideas that strengthen our community — especially events, collaborations, or initiatives that support our members. If you have suggestions, opportunities, or perspectives that can help us create value for Coaches across Denmark, I would love to hear from you.
